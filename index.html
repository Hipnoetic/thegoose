About us

We are a full service restaurant & pub located in beautiful Merrickville. Come enjoy yourself in our cozy home setting with your friends and family or just the two of you for a romantic night out. You can sit on our patio in the warm summer months and enjoy a cold pint. We have three big screens in our canadian pub to enjoy sporting events of all kinds. Book your next party in our full service private gathering room. Good food and good cheer awaits all. Cheers!!!

THE PUB

You can look into a towns soul by sitting in one of it's pubs. This said, Merrickville is a great town full of warm and inviting people ready to chat with anyone! Belly up to the bar, enjoy a refreshing beverage and the company of friendly locals, or you might like a little privacy which you can enjoy in one of the booths in our pub. Cheers!!

DINING ROOM

Come in and sit down in our cozy, warm, inviting dinning room and enjoy a great homemade meal! Order and relax, we are waiting to prepare the right dish for you! You'll feel right at home! Bring your family or that someone special for great ambience and a great meal at The Goose!!




EVENT ROOM

If you have an event planned ask us to host your get together in our beautiful banquet room! Birthdays, meetings what ever kind of event or party you might have in mind we can accommodate your special day. You can order off the menu or have us prepare something special for your event! We are here to help you with your plans! See you soon!!
